# CNIPA Module

Use the current Patent Law, Implementing Regulations, Patent Examination Guidelines base text, and amendments effective on the response date. As of this package build, the 2025 Guidelines amendment took effect on 2026-01-01.

## Record fields

- 通知书类型、发文日、答复期限原文和页码。
- 涉及的每项权利要求、法条、引证文件和审查员事实认定。
- 当前有效权利要求文本及历次修改。

## Substantive routing

- 新颖性、创造性和实用性: route under Patent Law Article 22 and the current Guidelines.
- 说明书充分公开: route under Article 26(3).
- 权利要求以说明书为依据、清楚简要: route under Article 26(4).
- 修改超范围: route under Article 33 and the current Implementing Regulations and Guidelines.
- 算法、商业规则、人工智能或比特流相关申请: load the current special sections before analysis.

## Amendment basis

For every added limitation, cite the as-filed claim, description paragraph, drawing, or an explicit combination. Explain why the amended subject matter is directly derivable from the original disclosure. A feature assembled from separate embodiments requires a specific compatibility analysis.

The response-stage amendment must target a defect identified in the communication. Record replacement pages and comparison materials required by the current filing system, but leave final submission-format verification to the filing professional.

## Inventive step

Record:

1. closest prior art used by the examiner;
2. distinguishing features as a whole;
3. technical problem supported by the application and differences;
4. technical effects;
5. examiner's combination or common-knowledge rationale;
6. evidence for common knowledge where the disputed feature contributes to solving the technical problem;
7. applicant's traverse or supported amendment.

Do not reduce the assessment to the number of distinguishing features or claim breadth.

## Interaction

Use 会晤、电话讨论 or another currently available mechanism only after checking the current Guidelines. Any agreed substantive amendment is reflected in the formally submitted written document.
