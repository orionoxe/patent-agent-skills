# Strategy Gates

Apply these gates before choosing a response.

## Gate 1: Record accuracy

- Is the quoted claim text current?
- Does the examiner cite the passage asserted?
- Is the reference date, family member, and translation correct?
- Is the rejection procedural posture accurately identified?

An inaccurate record is corrected before strategy is chosen.

## Gate 2: Pure traverse

Prefer a traverse candidate when the examiner's mapping lacks a limitation, relies on an unsupported interpretation, treats a result as a disclosed mechanism, or uses a combination without the jurisdiction-required rationale.

Record:

- strongest missing limitation;
- exact source and pinpoint;
- likely examiner counterargument;
- whether clarification would narrow the record.

## Gate 3: Amendment

An amendment candidate needs all of:

- clear original-disclosure basis under the selected jurisdiction;
- a defined defect it cures;
- a claim-chart reason the new limitation matters;
- acceptable commercial scope;
- consistency with dependent claims and other claim categories;
- a record of admission and estoppel risks for reviewer assessment.

If one item is missing, label the amendment [NO-BASIS] or [STRATEGY-DECISION].

## Gate 4: Evidence

Evidence or declaration candidates identify:

- proposition to prove;
- witness or source;
- data provenance and reproducibility;
- nexus to the claimed subject matter;
- procedural admissibility and timing;
- statements requiring legal verification.

No result, citation, market fact, or expert view is invented to complete a draft.

## Gate 5: Interview or oral proceedings

Prepare:

- objective;
- two or three decisive issues;
- primary proposal and fallback;
- non-negotiable scope;
- exact questions;
- record-making requirement after the interaction.

Use the jurisdiction's actual mechanism and terminology.

## Gate 6: Filing-draft separation

The filing draft contains only the selected position. The internal strategy memo contains rejected alternatives, risk analysis, probabilities, and negotiation limits.
